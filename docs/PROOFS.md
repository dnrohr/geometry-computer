# Operation proofs

Proof records contain a title, intuition, givens, assumptions, linked claims, and conclusion. Addition/subtraction rely on rigid length transfer. Multiplication and division use corresponding ratios of similar triangles, with division assuming `y ≠ 0`. Square root uses the semicircle altitude theorem and assumes `x ≥ 0`.

Proof cards open by click or keyboard, so hover is never required. Focusing or hovering a claim highlights the geometry IDs supporting it.
